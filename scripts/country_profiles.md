Argentina is a "cost-of-living squeeze" segment rather than a "debt crisis" segment: users avoid overspending and debt, but housing costs structurally eat into disposable income more than any other market, correlating with the lowest satisfaction scores. Product messaging should prioritize housing-cost budgeting and rent/mortgage optimization over debt-management features.
